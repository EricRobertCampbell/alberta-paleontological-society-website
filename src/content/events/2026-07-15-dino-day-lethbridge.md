We are excited to be teaming up with Legend Art Gallery in Lethbridge, who will be hosting us for a day of touching fossils and creating dinosaur art!

**Celebrate 30 Years of Dinosaur Discovery!**

Get ready for a ROAR-some adventure as Legend Art Gallery partners with the Devil's Coulee Dinosaur Museum to bring prehistoric fun to the heart of Lethbridge. Join us for an unforgettable Dino Day, where dinosaurs, art, creativity, and hands-on learning come together for an exciting family experience. Whether your child dreams of becoming a paleontologist, loves dinosaurs, or simply enjoys creating art, this event is packed with activities that will spark curiosity and imagination.

**When:** Wednesday, July 15, 2026, 11:00 AM – 5:00 PM  
**Where:** Legend Art Gallery, 519 7 Street South, Lethbridge, AB  
**Admission:** $36 per group (up to 6 people)

Tickets and registration are available on [Eventbrite](https://www.eventbrite.ca/e/dino-day-tickets-1992782179142).
