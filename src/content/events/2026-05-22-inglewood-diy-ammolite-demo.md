Join the Alberta Fossil Family Ammolite Group for an engaging, hands-on experience where you will learn how ammolite is found, mined, and uncovered. Explore Alberta’s iconic gemstone through demonstrations and conversation.

This is an all-ages session for rock hunters, fossil enthusiasts, artists, and anyone curious about this rare gemstone resource unique to Alberta. Participants can ask questions and learn about the roughly 65-million-year-old ammonite fossils that make ammolite so extraordinary.

Part of the [InglewooDIY Festival](https://www.inglewoodyyc.ca/inglewoodiy-festival) (May 22–31, 2026).

**When:** Friday, May 22, 2026 — 6:30–8:00 p.m.  
**Where:** YW Community Hub, 1715 17 Ave SE, Calgary

Workshop registration and updates are handled through the festival; see **[InglewooDIY Festival — workshop listings](https://www.inglewoodyyc.ca/inglewoodiy-festival)** for signup details.
