Come to the museum for **FREE** kids camps this spring break. These camps are 2 days long and will be hosted on site at the museum. We are limited to **12 participants** per camp, so sign up today!

**March 21–22: Ages 6–10**

Come learn about fossils, dinosaurs, and palaeoecology! What dinosaurs lived in Cretaceous Tumbler Ridge? What did they eat? How did they spend their time? Let's find out by looking at fossils and other evidence of ancient life!

For more information and to sign up, visit the [Tumbler Ridge Museum Foundation](https://www.trmf.ca/).
