Save the date! The **XVI International Palynological Congress (IPC)** and **XII International Organisation of Palaeobotany (IOPC)** joint meeting will be held in Calgary, August 7–11, 2028.

The dates were selected to coincide with the [International Geological Congress (IGC 2028)](https://igc2028canada.com/), also to be held in Calgary in August 2028. Calgary is close to multiple UNESCO World Heritage sites, and field excursions to some of them are being planned.

Paleobotanists and palynologists from around the world—working in academia, government, non-governmental organizations, and industry—will gather to present and discuss current research.

## Key dates

- **Call for abstracts:** August 2027
- **Registration opens:** October 2027
- **Preliminary program released:** May 2028

## Organizing committee

Manuel Bringué (Canadian Association of Palynologists) and Christopher West (Royal Tyrrell Museum of Palaeontology) co-chair the local organizing committee. Jennifer Galloway and Kimberly Bell serve on the organizing committee for the IPC side.

If you have ideas for sessions or workshops, or want to be involved in any capacity, please reach out to the organizers via [the conference website](https://calgary2028.com/).

For updates, join the [XVI IPC–XII IOPC mailing list](https://calgary2028.com/).
