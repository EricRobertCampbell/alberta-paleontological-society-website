## Trivia-saurus Weekend at the Tumbler Ridge Museum

Come to the museum this weekend for **Trivia-saurus**! This event is **free with admission** – come as a group or play solo and see how much you know.

Join the museum for **four rounds of dino trivia**:

- **Dinos Big and Small**
- **Once Upon a Time**
- **Human Meets Dinosaur**
- **Dinos on Screen**

**Date:** Saturday, March 14, 2026  
**Time:** 1:00pm–2:30pm  
**Location:** Tumbler Ridge Museum, Tumbler Ridge, BC

For more details and other museum programs, visit the Tumbler Ridge Museum events page.
