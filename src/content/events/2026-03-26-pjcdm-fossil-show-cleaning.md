This Spring Break, bring your kids to the Dinosaur Museum for some prehistoric fun! Kids will get to explore fossils from our world-famous bonebed, learn how these dinosaur bones fossilized over 65 million years, and then try their hand at fossil cleaning (just like a palaeontologist)!

**When:** Thursday, March 26, 2026, 2:00 PM – 4:00 PM  
**Where:** Philip J. Currie Dinosaur Museum, Wembley, AB  
**Ages:** 6+  
**Cost:** $15 per person (includes museum admission)  
**Duration:** 2 hours | Max 20 participants
