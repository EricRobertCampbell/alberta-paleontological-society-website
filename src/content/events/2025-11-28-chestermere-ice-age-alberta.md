Join us for a fascinating journey back in time to Alberta's Ice Age like you have never seen before with Sandstone Prehistoric Safaris! Together we will uncover what an Ice age is, discover what wildlife roamed in Alberta's Ice Age savannah, what happened to them and what studying the past can tell us about our future.

**This program is geared toward families and children ages 6+ but the whole family is welcome to attend.**

Sponsored by Chestermere Historical Foundation.

**Date:** Friday, November 28, 2025  
**Time:** 4:00 p.m. - 5:00 p.m.  
**Location:** Library Program Room, Chestermere Public Library

Registration required.

## Registration information:

- Please note that participants must register for each program date separately with one participant per registration form. Parents/Guardians attending must also complete a registration form.
- Missing two registered programs in a session without canceling or notifying Library staff will result in the participant's removal from remaining programs in that session.
- Parents / guardians must stay in the library if the child is under 8 years old.
