Palaeo Palooza is a fun, family-friendly science festival held annually on the second weekend of August, from 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM on both Saturday and Sunday. With a carnival-style atmosphere, it is an exciting opportunity for all ages to explore science through games, activities, and hands-on learning. This year, the event will take place on August 8 and 9.

Join us for our 2-day Science Festival, jam-packed with dino-riffic family fun! The event is on August 8th and 9th from 10 am to 5 pm.

Enjoy carnival games, an inflatable obstacle course, geode smash, dino shoot, a palaeo-themed makers space, a scavenger hunt, special screenings, free bus service, and many more. It's a weekend of prehistoric family fun!

**When:** Saturday and Sunday, August 8–9, 2026, 10:00 AM – 5:00 PM  
**Where:** Philip J. Currie Dinosaur Museum, Wembley, AB

Tickets are available to purchase at [dinomuseum.ca](https://dinomuseum.ca).
