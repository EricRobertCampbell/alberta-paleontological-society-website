Calling all dino-riffic families! We are hosting **Prehistoric Saturdays** again at the Dino Museum!

Come spend the day at the museum and participate in a fun palaeo activity. Plan your day around Cafe on 43 lunch and our reptile show at 1 pm.

**February 28: Palaeo Magnet Making!** Drop in from 1 pm – 4 pm. Paint and assemble your own dino magnet. Choose from 4 types of plaster replicas: dino foot, trilobite, ammonite, and a mini dinosaur skeleton.

**Cost:** $5 with museum admission  
**Ages:** 5+
