The Canadian Paleontology Conference (CPC 2026) is hosted by the [Geological Association of Canada’s Paleontology Division (GAC-PD)](https://www.gacpd.ca/). The meeting runs Monday, August 24th to Wednesday, August 26th, 2026, centred at the University of Regina, Regina, Saskatchewan.

Scientific sessions (oral and poster presentations) are planned August 25 and 26. An icebreaker will be held at the Royal Saskatchewan Museum on the evening of August 24. An awards banquet and field trips are also being planned; further detail will appear in the second circular.

**Registration and abstracts**

- Registration opens: May 1, 2026  
- Abstract submission deadline: Monday, June 1, 2026  
- Registration and abstract submission will be handled through the FourWaves platform; instructions will follow by email. Advance registration is strongly encouraged (on-site registration will be available).  
- Questions: [gac.paleontology@gmail.com](mailto:gac.paleontology@gmail.com)

**Billings Award**

The Billings Award recognizes outstanding contributions to Canadian palaeontology. Nominations are encouraged; nomination packages and instructions are described in [the first circular (PDF)](/events/2026/external/CPC%202026%20First%20Circular.pdf). Nomination deadline: July 6, 2026.

Anyone may submit an abstract and attend; students are especially encouraged to participate and gain presentation experience.

For updates, see the [CPC page on the GAC-PD site](https://www.gacpd.ca/cpc).
