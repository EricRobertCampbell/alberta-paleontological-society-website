The Alberta Palaeontological Society is excited to announce our field trip schedule for 2026! This year's lineup includes:

- **Saturday, June 6, 2026**: Royal Alberta Museum Tour, Edmonton
- **Sunday, July 12, 2026**: Stonerose Centre Trip, Republic, Washington
- **Saturday, July 18, 2026**: Knudsen's Farm Field Trip, Huxley, AB
- **Saturday, August 8, 2026**: Canyon Creek Field Trip, Kananaskis Country, AB
- **Saturday, August 15, 2026**: Tyndall Stone Tour, Calgary, AB
- **Saturday, August 22, 2026**: Pipestone Creek Bonebed Tour and Excavation, Wembley, AB
- **Sunday–Monday, August 23–24, 2026**: Tumbler Ridge Trip, Tumbler Ridge, BC

For detailed descriptions, registration deadlines, and the sign-up form, see the [Field Trips page](/events/fieldtrips/).
